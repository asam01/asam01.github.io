Multi-Agent Collaboration

Efficient Speech Emotion Recognition
------
In this project, I am exploring methods to improve both train-time and test-time efficiency in speech emotion recognition by exploring PEFT techniques such as LoRA.


Emotion Recognition from Unstructured Speech
------
*This project is in collaboration with Butler Hospital's psychiatry group.* In this project, I am developing modeling techniques to predict daily self-report emotion measures from participant's in-the-wild, personal phone calls.
